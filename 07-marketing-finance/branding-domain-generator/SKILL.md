# Branding & Domain Generator

Creative naming and domain strategy tool.

## Core Capabilities

- Domain name generation
- Availability suggestions
- Brand analysis
- Naming strategies

## When to Use

- Naming startup
- Launching product
- Rebranding
- Personal brand

## TLD Knowledge

- .com → global trust
- .io → tech
- .ai → AI products
- .dev → developers
- .app → applications

## Naming Strategies

- Short and memorable
- Easy pronunciation
- Brandable words
- Avoid numbers

## Output Style

- 10-15 domain ideas
- Categorized suggestions
- Branding explanations
